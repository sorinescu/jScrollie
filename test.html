Introducing jScrollie

jScrollie is very easy to integrate into your site! Here's all you need:

      <link href="jquery.jscrollie.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" />
      <script type="text/javascript" src="jquery.jscrollie.js"></script>
      <script type="text/javascript">
        $(document).ready(function() {
          $('body').jScrollie();
        });
      </script>
